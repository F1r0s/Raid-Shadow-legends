How to Redeem Plarium Promo Codes on Mobile (iOS & Android)

This is the fastest method to redeem raid promo codes — the entire process takes under 30 seconds on mobile.

1

Open Raid: Shadow Legends

Launch the game on your iOS or Android device. Wait for the game to fully load to the Bastion screen (your main hub). Do not tap anything else yet.

2

Tap the ☰ Menu Icon (Top-Left Corner)

Look for the three horizontal lines (☰) in the very top-left corner of your screen. Tap it to open the main game menu. This is sometimes called the "hamburger menu."

3

Select "Promo Codes" from the Menu

Scroll down through the menu options until you see "Promo Codes." Tap it. A dialog box will pop up with a text input field ready for your code.

4

Enter Your Raid Shadow Legends Code

Tap the text field ("Enter code here") and type or paste your promo code exactly as shown above. Codes are case-sensitive — copy them directly from our homepage codes list to avoid typos.

5

Tap "Submit" — Collect Your Rewards

Tap the "Submit" button. A success confirmation will appear. Open your in-game Inbox (envelope icon) to collect your free rewards immediately.

Pro tip: Long-press on a code on our homepage to copy it instantly, then switch to Raid and paste it directly. This avoids any manual typing errors that cause "Code Not Found" errors.

How to Redeem Raid Promo Codes on PC (Plarium Play)

Redeeming Plarium promo codes on PC uses the Plarium Play desktop client. The menu is in exactly the same location as mobile, making the process identical.

1

Launch Plarium Play Desktop App

Open the Plarium Play launcher on your Windows PC. If you don't have it installed, download it free from plarium.com. Sign into your Plarium account.

2

Start Raid: Shadow Legends

From the Plarium Play library, click "Play" on Raid: Shadow Legends and wait for the game to fully load to the Bastion screen.

3

Click the Menu Icon (☰) in the Top-Left

The three-line menu (☰) is in the exact same position as mobile — top-left corner of the game window. Click it to open the main menu.

4

Click "Promo Codes" and Enter Your Code

Scroll down in the menu to find "Promo Codes." Click it, then paste your copied code into the text box. Use Ctrl+V to paste on PC.

5

Click "Submit" to Claim Your Free Rewards

Click "Submit" and you'll see a confirmation message. Your rewards are instantly delivered — click the Inbox icon to collect them.

Frequently Asked Questions About Redeeming Raid Codes

Can I redeem Raid promo codes on multiple accounts?

Yes — Plarium promo codes can be redeemed on multiple different Plarium accounts. Each unique account gets one redemption per code. You cannot redeem the same code twice on the same account.

How long do Plarium promo codes last?

Most Raid Shadow Legends codes last 24–72 hours. Content creator partnership codes can sometimes last up to a week. Event-specific codes tied to Plarium's seasonal promotions may also have extended validity windows.

Where is the promo code menu in Raid: Shadow Legends?

Open the game to the Bastion screen, tap the ☰ icon in the top-left corner, then scroll down to find "Promo Codes." This location is identical on iOS, Android, and PC via Plarium Play.

Do codes work on Plarium Play (PC) the same as mobile?

Yes — all Plarium promo codes work on PC via Plarium Play exactly the same way as on mobile. Rewards are tied to your Plarium account, not your device, so you'll see them on all platforms after redeeming on any one device.
